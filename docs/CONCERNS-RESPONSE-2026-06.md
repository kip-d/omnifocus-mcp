## For fork maintainers

If you are running a fork ahead of upstream fixes, the two items most worth patching locally are the complex-`NOT`
silent pass-through (OMN-131 — until fixed, avoid `NOT` payloads other than `NOT: {status: ...}`; for tag exclusion, the
intended path is the tags `none` operator — though note the result-correctness coverage caveat in the Test Coverage Gaps
section applies to it) and awareness that `smart_suggest` returns available next actions, not urgency-ranked results.
Everything in the **Accepted** rows is a deliberate tradeoff you may weigh differently — the rationales above are the
complete reasoning, not a summary of a longer private discussion.
